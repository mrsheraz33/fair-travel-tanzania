WHO ARE WE

Tanzania Travel Volunteer is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering community development through education, healthcare, and environmental initiatives in Moshi, Kilimanjaro region. We connect passionate volunteers with meaningful projects that create lasting positive impact.
