Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Pick up from airport, safari briefing, overnight at Arusha hotel.

Day 2: Tarangire National Park

Famous for elephants and baobab trees. Game drive.

Day 3: Serengeti National Park

Endless plains, big cats, possible wildebeest migration.

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater

Descend into the crater — best chance for black rhino.

Day 5: Lake Manyara → Departure

Tree-climbing lions, flamingos, then transfer to airport.
