Information & FAQ

Everything you need to know before your volunteer journey

Additional Information

Important details to help you prepare for your volunteer experience in Tanzania

Arrival & Orientation

All volunteers receive an orientation after arrival covering:

  • Local culture and customs
  • Safety guidelines
  • Program expectations
  • Transportation information
  • Basic Swahili phrases

Internet & Communication

Wi-Fi and mobile internet are available in most areas. Local SIM cards such as:

  • Vodacom
  • Airtel
  • Halotel

can easily be purchased after arrival.

Transportation

Our team can assist with:

  • Airport transfers
  • Bus bookings
  • Local transportation
  • Safari and trekking connections

Health & Vaccinations

We recommend travelers consult their doctor before traveling to Tanzania regarding:

  • Routine vaccinations
  • Malaria prevention
  • Travel insurance

Travel insurance is highly recommended for all volunteers.

Responsible Volunteering

We believe volunteering should support and empower local communities respectfully and sustainably. Volunteers are encouraged to:

  • Respect local culture
  • Dress modestly in community areas
  • Take part with an open mind
  • Support ethical tourism practices

Cultural Experience

Volunteers have opportunities to experience:

  • Tanzanian food
  • Maasai culture
  • Local markets
  • Coffee tours
  • Waterfalls
  • Traditional dance and music

Weekend Activities

During free time, volunteers can enjoy:

  • Safari adventures
  • Mount Kilimanjaro trekking
  • Mount Meru trekking
  • Hot springs
  • Zanzibar trips
  • Cycling tours
  • Cultural village visits

Donation Support

If you wish to support local communities further, donations such as:

  • School supplies
  • Sports equipment
  • Educational materials
  • Clothes for children

are always appreciated but completely optional.

Sustainability Commitment

Part of every booking helps support:

  • Children's education
  • Community development
  • Local employment
  • Environmental conservation initiatives

Emergency Support

Our local team is available to assist volunteers during emergencies or unexpected situations throughout their stay in Tanzania.

Photography & Social Media

We encourage respectful photography. Please always ask permission before taking close photos of local people or children.

Payment & Booking Policy

To secure your placement, an advance deposit may be required. The remaining balance can usually be paid upon arrival unless otherwise agreed.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation terms vary depending on the program and travel services booked. Full details will be provided before confirmation.

Our Mission

At Tanzania Travel Volunteers, our mission is to connect travelers with meaningful experiences that create positive impact for both visitors and local communities.

Travel with Purpose, Leave a Positive Impact.
