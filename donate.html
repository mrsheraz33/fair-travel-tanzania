Mussa Mushi

Founder of Tanzania Travel Volunteer

Mussa Mushi is the founder of Tanzania Travel Volunteer, an organization aimed at providing volunteering opportunities and community development initiatives in Tanzania. Tanzania Travel Volunteer focuses on projects that support local communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable development efforts.

Mussa Mushi's work involves coordinating with international volunteers and connecting them with projects that match their skills and interests, which can range from teaching and healthcare to environmental conservation. The mission is to encourage cultural exchange and promote positive impacts on local communities through volunteer work.

VISIT

Karibu Tanzania!

We welcome visitors to our centre in Moshi, Northern Tanzania. Enjoy a tour of the centre, learning more about our programmes, meeting the students and staff. Taste a traditional Tanzanian meal while sharing lunch with our young beneficiaries.

If you would like to visit the home of one of our youth, this can be arranged through our Social Worker and is a wonderful opportunity to understand more about the Tanzanian way of life.

SAFARI TIME

In partnership with our friends at Kilimanjaro Safari Bookings we show our visitors the highlights of Tanzania, including Mount Kilimanjaro Trekking, Mount Meru Trekking the plains of the Serengeti, the rich wildlife of the Ngorongoro Crater and the aqua blue sea of the exotic spice islands of Zanzibar.

Kilimanjaro Safari Bookings donate generously to Tanzania Travel Volunteer, providing much needed general support. $50 per client is gifted directly to Tanzania Travel Volunteer

If you want to learn more about a trip of a lifetime to Tanzania, please contact us.
