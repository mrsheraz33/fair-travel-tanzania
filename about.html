About Fair Travel

Our story, mission, and commitment to Tanzania

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Fair Travel Tanzania began with a simple idea: travel should benefit the places we visit, not exploit them. What started as a small team of passionate guides has grown into Tanzania's leading non-profit tour operator.

We've guided thousands of travelers to the summit of Kilimanjaro, across the Serengeti plains, and through the stone streets of Zanzibar — all while ensuring our footprint leaves only positive impact.

Today, we're proud to be recommended by Bradt Travel Guides and trusted by adventurers worldwide.

Meet Our Founder

Mussa Mushi, founder and head guide, has over 20 years of experience leading expeditions across Tanzania. His deep local knowledge, commitment to safety, and passion for conservation drive everything we do.
