ABOUT US

Volunteer in Tanzania and Empower Communities

Tanzania Travel Volunteer connects passionate volunteers from around the world with local communities in Moshi, Kilimanjaro. We are dedicated to creating meaningful impact through education, healthcare, community development, and environmental conservation.

Our Key Areas of Focus

Education

Teaching English, Mathematics, Science and adult literacy programs in local schools.

Healthcare

Supporting local clinics, maternal health, HIV/AIDS awareness and basic medical care.

Community Development

Infrastructure projects, women's empowerment, water & sanitation initiatives.

Environmental Conservation

Tree planting, wildlife awareness and sustainable farming practices.
